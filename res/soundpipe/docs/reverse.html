reverse

Signal reverser
Reverse will store a signal inside a buffer and play it back reversed.

sp_reverse_destroy(sp_reverse **reverse)

Mandatory Parameters

delay: Delay time in seconds.
(Recommended value: 1.0)

Inputs

input: Signal input.

Outputs

out: Signal output.
