Ethersurface

Ethersurface is a musical app for the android device. Users can slide their fingers across the surface to create sounds. The X-axis control pitch, while the Y-axis controls timbre. Ethersurface comes with a menu that allows users to change octave, range, sound, and scale (there is even an option for the 13-step Bohlen Pierce scale)

All the sounds you hear are custom built instruments synthesized in realtime with the Csound API.

Download the app for free at the Google Play store.

You can also browse the source code on github.
