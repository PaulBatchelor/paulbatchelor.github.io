Anthropomorphic Sound

Anthropomorphic Sound, or "anthrosound", describes any sound that is non-human in origin, yet has human-like characteristics attributed to it.

Related phrases include "Sounds with Faces" and "Sounds with Personality", and is closely related to the sonic version of Pareidolia, the tendenancy to see faces in inanimate objects.

Anthrosound is core focus of the gestlings project.
