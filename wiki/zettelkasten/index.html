Zettelkasten

keywords: zet, knowledge, mindmap, organization A zettelkasten is a knowledge management system.

Traditionally, a zettelkasten consists of a set of physical index cards which store small bits of information and usually have some sort of indexing system for cataloguing.

In these systems, cards are able to reference other cards. As zettelkastens grow in size and complexity, interesting relationships and patterns emerge from the connections.
