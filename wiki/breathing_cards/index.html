The Breathing Cards

The breathing cards are a series of short audio-visual etudes. I call them "cards" because of their small size being like postcards. Cards are breathing because they are temporal (audio + video), rather than static.

In many ways, this is a distant relative to the sporthlings series.

Each video is produced using 128x64 sized canvas, with magnification applied in post.

The soundtrack is of equal (if not more) importance than what is being seen visually. The idea is to have sounds "bleed" onto the visual canvas.

Cards are made using the monolith computer music ecosystem.
