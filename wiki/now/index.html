Here's what I am doing/working on as of November 6th, 2021:

Looptober is over, now it's time for novembeat_2021.

Working on a new blog series: Synthwave From Scratch. Hoping to have at least the first 2 or 3 parts done this month. We shall see.

Thanks to Novembeat, I'm thinking more seriously about sample curation, and am actively improving the tools surrounding that, including zet and crate. I have a notion to begin publishing the static wiki component of my sample library zettelkasten.

Looking into making improvements to my singing synthesizer, as I started hitting creative walls with the current iteration during looptober_2021. Things like Velum will be easy to add from Voc, but I will also be looking into how to do more things related to Fricatives. The big thing here is synchronized control, which I am currently doing via gest. These added components may add more complexity.

Might make a singing synthesizer based on Klatt Synthesis controllable by gest. Should be a great deal more efficient, which would allow it to run on underpowered devices, which falls in line more with my toys_not_tools mantra.
