Here's what I am doing/working on as of October 25th, 2021:

October has been a month doing looptober. I've been using it as an opportunity to try out my gesture synthesizer with some of my vocal synthesizer components tract and voc.

Thinking about Rust and Audio DSP, and porting some of my algorithms to that language. I have no plans on abandoning the C programming language for projects like sndkit. But I'd like to see how the rest of the world lives, and perhaps think about this landscape in the future?

Getting back into wavetable synthesis and FM. The missing link was gest, which now allows me to morph between waveforms in any arbitrary size and number. Implementing a 4-operater FM patch for sndkitwith morphing capabilities seems inevitable.

In general, lots of time, thought, and energy spent on gest, as well as thinking about gesture as a general concept.

Working on a new blog series: Synthwave From Scratch. I made a synthwave track using sndkit and gest, and I want to talk about how it was made.
