Prelude

Bitlang is a tiny stack-based DSL, implemented in ANSI-C, designed for the sole purpose of producing procedurally generated 1-bit art. The goal was to build a system that could efficiently be used in the context of video and animation for projects of mine like the Candy Crystal Rainbow Codex.
