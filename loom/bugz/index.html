Prelude

At the time of writing this prelude, Bugz is only a hypothetical. Little to no code has been written, but IMO the writing itself outlines a pretty solid plan. This program is my attempt to make amends with my prejudices towards granular synthesis and actually explore the technique on my own terms. As I mention in the overview, this is a fairly naive attempt at implementing granular synthesis, so there's a non-zero chance this will be a project that doesn't go anywhere.
