Ars Brevis

Ars Brevis is a computer music composition of a poem set to music. The poem is written by Piet Hein, and it is part of a poetry/doodle anthology called Grooks:

ARS BREVIS
By Piet Hein

There is
one art,
no more,
no less,
to do
all things
with art-
lessness.

The piece is divided up into three movements, each with a different person reciting the poem.

Ars Brevis was created entirely in Csound.
