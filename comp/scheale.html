Scheale

Scheale is a generative ambient composition, written in my music language Sporth.

It is the first attempt at composition using this language, and therefore the first Sporth composition to ever exist in this universe.

My first stab at composing in Sporth was an interesting and fun challenge. All sounds and note events were created through routing unit generators together, similar to how a composition would be realized on a modular synthesizer. It was very easy to build up very complicated patches. Even a few lines of Sporth code could yield very hard to follow patches! For this reason, each instrument was split up into different Sporth patches, where they were daisy-chained together in serial.
