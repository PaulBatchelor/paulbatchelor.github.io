36. phasor

phasor creates a phasor signal, which is a linar ramp that goes from 0 to 1 at a frequency specified in freq. A phasor has many useful purposes, but it is most often used when constructing table-lookup oscillators. iphs is the initial phase.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (phasor freq iphs)
  (param freq)
  (param iphs)
  (rvl "phasor"))
