111. bitnoise

A 1-bit noise generator, inspired by the NES APU.


This generator takes in two arguments: a mode flag and a rate variable. mode changes the behavior of the inteneral LFSR. Rate changes the speed of the noise generator (therefore the tone quality).

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (bitnoise rate mode)
  (param rate)
  (param mode)
  (rvl "bitnoise"))
