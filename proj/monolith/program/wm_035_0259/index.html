61. eqfil

eqfil applies a equalizing peak filter to a signal in. The center frequency is freq, with a bandwidth bw and gain gain. A gain value greater than 1 is a boost, less than 1 is a cut.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (eqfil in freq bw gain)
  (param in)
  (param freq)
  (param bw)
  (param gain)
  (rvl "eqfil"))
