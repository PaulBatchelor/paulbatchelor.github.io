98. smoothdelay

smoothdelay is a adjustable delay line that does not introduce pitch shifting artififacts. Instead, it implements a double buffer that it dynamically interpolates between at interpolation time interp (in units of sampels).

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (smoothdelay in fdbk del maxdel interp)
  (param in)
  (param fdbk)
  (param del)
  (param maxdel)
  (param interp)
  (rvl "smoothdelay"))
