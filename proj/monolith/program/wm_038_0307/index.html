73. revsc

revsc applies a stereo FDN reverb algorithm by Sean Costello to a stero input signal supplied by in1 and in2. fdbk controls the feedback amount (should be between 0 and 1). cutoff is the timbre control in range of Hz.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (revsc in1 in2 fdbk cutoff)
  (param in1)
  (param in2)
  (param fdbk)
  (param cutoff)
  (rvl "revsc"))
