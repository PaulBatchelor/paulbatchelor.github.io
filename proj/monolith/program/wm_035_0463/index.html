112. scrubby

scrubby is a frequency domain sample scrubber.

pos is the lookup position, in units of seconds.

amp is the amplitude.

pitch is the playback speed (1normal, 0.5half, 2=double, etc).

ft is a lambda that returns the ftable containing the sample.

winsize is the FFT window size. A sensible default is 1024.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (scrubby pos amp pitch ft winsize)
  (param pos)
  (param amp)
  (param pitch)
  (ft)
  (param winsize)
  (rvl "scrubby"))
