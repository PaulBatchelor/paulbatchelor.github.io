25. tenvx

tenvx is a triggerable envelope generator with exponential slope. in is expected to be a trigger signal. atk, hold, and rel are attack, hold, and release times, in units of seconds.

Because of how the internal algorithm works, hold >= atk.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (tenvx in atk hold rel)
  (param in)
  (param atk)
  (param hold)
  (param rel)
  (rvl "tenvx"))
