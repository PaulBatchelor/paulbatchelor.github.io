32. clock

clock creates a clock signal at a specified tempo in BPM, and a subdivision amount subdiv (a value of 4 is 16th notes, 2 eigth notes, etc). The tk paramter is used to reset the clock.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (clock tk tempo subdiv)
  (param tk)
  (param tempo)
  (param subdiv)
  (rvl "clock"))
