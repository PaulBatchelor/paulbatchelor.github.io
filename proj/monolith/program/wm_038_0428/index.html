103. tsmp

tsmp is a triggerable in-memory sample player with playback varispeed control.

in is a trigger signal.

play is the playback speed amount (1=normal, 0.5halfspeed, 2doublespeed, etc).

ft is a lambda that returns the ftable containing the sample.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (tsmp in play ft)
  (param in)
  (param play)
  (ft)
  (rvl "tsmp"))
