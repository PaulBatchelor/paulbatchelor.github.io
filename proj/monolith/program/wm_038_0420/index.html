101. pshift

pshift implements a time-based pitch shifter. in is the input signal. shift is the shift amount, in semitones. window and xfade are the window size and crossfade amount (in units of samples).

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (pshift in shift window xfade)
  (param in)
  (param shift)
  (param window)
  (param xfade)
  (rvl "pshift"))
