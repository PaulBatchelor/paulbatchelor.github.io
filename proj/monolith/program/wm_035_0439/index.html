111. voc

physical modelling vocal synthesizer.

freq is the fundamental frequency of the voice.

pos and diameter are high-level shaping controls of the vocal tract. Should be unit values.

tenseness adds tenseness. The closer this approaches 0, the more whisper there will be.

velum is typically treated as a toggle value, and it is used to get nasal frequencies.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (voc freq pos diameter tenseness velum)
    (param freq)
    (param pos)
    (param diameter)
    (param tenseness)
    (param velum)
    (rvl "voc"))
