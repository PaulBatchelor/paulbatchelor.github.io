66. fosc

fosc is a classic FM pair oscillator. amp is the amplitude. c, m, and i, are the C:M ratios and modulation index amount, respecitively. ftis a lambda that produces the ftable.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (fosc freq amp c m i ft)
  (param freq)
  (param amp)
  (param c)
  (param m)
  (param i)
  (ft)
  (rvl "fosc"))
