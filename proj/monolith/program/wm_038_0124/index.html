27. port

port is a portamento filter. It is a 1-pole smoothing filter designed to work on control signals. in is the input signal, and htime is known as the "half-time", which controls the smoothing amount (and can be approximately thought of as being in units of seconds). Larger htime means more smoothing.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (port in htime)
  (param in)
  (param htime)
  (rvl "port"))
