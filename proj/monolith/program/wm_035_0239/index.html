56. vdelay

vdelay is a variable delay line with feedback. It will take in a signal in, and delay it by delay seconds with some feedback amount feedback (should be >1). delay is an adjustable that should not exceeed maxdelay seconds. maxdelay must be a constant, and not a cable.

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (vdelay in feedback delay maxdelay)
  (param in)
  (param feedback)
  (param delay)
  (param maxdelay)
  (rvl "vdelay"))
