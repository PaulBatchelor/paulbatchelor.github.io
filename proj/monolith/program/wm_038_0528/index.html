128. fftcut

fftcut performs a hard lowpass filter cut on an ftable in the frequency domain. cut is the bin position for the cutoff point. This is inteded to be used to process waveforms with a light of high frequency aliasing content. It sort of works?

<<ugens-top>>=
(define (fftcut cut ft)
  (param cut)
  (ft)
  (rvl "fftcut"))
