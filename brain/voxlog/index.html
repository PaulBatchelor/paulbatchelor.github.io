What is a Voxlog?

Audio recordings of me talking to myself. Yup. I'm doing it.

low bitrate mp3 files are recorded and named, and then managed via a crate. These files are tagged under the voxlog group. Hopefully I will set up a review process that listens to these then takes notes, probably in a zetdoc format.

Play Stuff Quickly with Mplayer

You can review things in mplayer quickly with: 

mplayer -af scaletempo -speed 2.0 foo.mp3

This will play at 2x speed while preserving pitch.

A general procedure

Use u/voxlog_import to import audio files. In addition to creating an entry for the file, it will also create a zetdo task to review the file.

Reviewing at the moment means taking notes in the zetdoc format. I've found this requires a few passes of listening. The first pass is listening to the whole thing without notes (this can be done around 2x speed or so). The second review is when you take notes. Extract the meaningful ideas and the well articulated bits. Forget abou the rest.

Once the zetdoc has been imported, add a blurb to the UUID generated from it. Link that UUID to the audio file (which can be found from the task's UUID via u/linked_files.
