Voxlog

Audio recordings of me talking to myself. Yup. I'm doing it.

low bitrate mp3 files are recorded and named, and then managed via a crate. These files are tagged under the voxlog group. Hopefully I will set up a review process that listens to these then takes notes, probably in a zetdoc format.

You can review things in mplayer quickly with: 

mplayer -af scaletempo -speed 2.0 foo.mp3

This will play at 2x speed while preserving pitch.
