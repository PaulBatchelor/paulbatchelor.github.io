Inklings

Part of my note-taking system involves writing things by hand in ink. After that, they are scanned and processed manually, allowing me to briefly review ideas and concepts for a second time.

Some of these pages get set with a "blurb", or a brief description of what the content of the ink is. The ones that do get a blurb are displayed in the table below.

Note: only the blurbs and metadata are displayed, not the pages themselves.

Note: Timestamps are when the particular ink was imported into the database. Typically this is a bulk operation, so several will have very similar times. It still gives reasonably helpful chronology, so I am including it in the table.
