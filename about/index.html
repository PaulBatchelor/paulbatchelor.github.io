About

Hello! My name is Paul Batchelor. Allow me to talk about myself.

I would consider myself to be a sound designer, music technologist, and a half decent programmer and composer. In that order.

I was born in Boston, Massachusetts. In my youth, I studied classical and jazz bass. I attended the Berklee College of Music, where I graduated out of their Electronic Production and Design program. I am currently pursuing my Masters degree at CCRMA.

Some of my interests include Linux, Raspberry Pi computers, live coding, generative composition, and the Unix philosophy.
