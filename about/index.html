Introduction

Hi. My name is Paul Batchelor. That's my face up there.

I hold a masters degree from the Center for Computer Research in Music and Acoustics (CCRMA), and a undergraduate degree from the Berklee College of Music, where I majored in Electronic Production and Design.

I am a music technologist who enjoys exploring the various ways computation and computational devices can be used to facillitate creative artistic expression. It is a adolescent medium, which I believe has deep potential for bringing positive social change at an enormous scale. As a musician, I try to focus on the ways computers can produce novel sounds as well as how they can assist in music composition process.

Here are some things that get me excited:

  • Generative Composition
  • Domain-specific languages and notation systems
  • Audio Signal Processing
  • Linux and FOSS
  • Embedded Systems
  • Computer Graphics
  • Demoscene
