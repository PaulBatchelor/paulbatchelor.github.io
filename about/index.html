About

Hi. My name is Paul.

Most of my time is spent hunched over a computer (usually my computer), occasionally muttering to myself about this or that. On good days, I create music. To distract myself from making music, I create music software, in a process I often refer to as "teaching computers how to sing".

Do you ever think about what it means to be a computer? To compute? Or ever wonder what it means for a computer to be an "artistic medium"? And while we're on that subject, what is this "computer music" stuff, anyways?

Here are some things that get me excited:

  • Generative Composition
  • Modular Synthesis Environments
  • Domain-specific languages and notation systems
  • Audio DSP
  • Linux and FOSS
  • Embedded Systems
  • Computer Graphics
